Scoring & risk model

  • Quality score rewards topical fit, target-page presence, anchor themes and editorial / digital-PR / broken-link / unlinked-mention plays.
  • Risk flags fire when paid links miss rel=sponsored, when UGC/forum links miss rel=ugc or nofollow, on niche edits, and on missing topic/target/keywords.
  • Anchor guidance follows Google's link spam policy: avoid exact-match over-optimization, link schemes and undisclosed paid links.

Google-compliant defaults

  • Paid / sponsored placements must use rel="sponsored".
  • User-generated or forum links should use rel="ugc" or rel="nofollow".
  • Editorial, resource and reclamation links are typically follow.
  • Diversify anchor text: branded, naked URL, partial-match, generic; avoid stuffed exact-match.
